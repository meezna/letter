Do they overwhelm you when you’re trying to stay grounded?

Does my presence,
the way I show up for you,
the way I feel for you—

I will understand.

But if I could still be by your side,
even just as that close friend—
the one you can joke with about anything,
laugh with in midnight messages,
confess the random silly things to,

then that would mean the world to me.

I never asked to love you.
And I know you never asked to be loved by me.
But here we are.

I cherish every word you’ve ever said

I still do.

But I need to ask these things.
Not because I doubt us—
but because I care enough to protect what we have,
even if that means redefining it.

I will never stop respecting the life you’ve built.
And I will never stop being grateful for the space you gave me in your heart.

So please—tell me honestly,
Is my love a weight for you?
Or a warmth?

I’ll always be here.
As a best friend.
Or whatever makes your heart lightest.

Yours,
